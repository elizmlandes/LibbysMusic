Libby's Top Charts

A breakdown of some of my favorite music makers.

Khruangbin

Khruangbin is my favorite band. They blend genres so smoothly. Great for any occasion, and just about everyone that hears their tunes can't get enough. Laura Lee, on bass, is such a babe.

Kelsey Lu

Kelsey Lu sings beutifully and plays the cello. Her songs are raw and full of emotion.

Four Tet

Four Tet is a solo artist from London. He makes music that is great for focus on tasks, like coding. :)
